GERALD TOLOMEO, A New York/Metro area designer gains recognition not only from quality interiors and an impressive clientele, but also from work published in several national magazines and books. Although his commissions tend to be in the residential realm, other projects have included boutique restaurants, hotels and executive offices.

Mr. Tolomeo has participated in over fourteen designer show houses, including the prestigious Kips Bay Show House, NYC. A graduate of the New York School of interior Design and the Fashion Institute of Technology, NYC, he holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree from Montclair State University and has attended the Sorbonne, University of Paris (Histoire Des Beaux Arts)

Gerald Charles Tolomeo Ltd. collaborates with clients to visualize, collaborate and develop a client's personal tastes and vision. Through extensive resources, talented craftsmen and a pulse on what's new, they precisely tailor their work to fit the architecture and spatial circumstances of each project. With extensive knowledge of the design world, Tolomeo and his associates create dramatic and elegant interiors in a timely and professional manner.

Mr Tolomeo was honored as one of New York Spaces Magazine's 50 Top Designers three years in a row.
